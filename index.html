What counts as waste? Non-useful meeting time, attendance by people who do not need to be there, and post-meeting context recovery. Salary cost is estimated from annual salary ÷ 1,920 working hours. This is deliberately a direct-cost model: employer taxes, benefits, overhead, and opportunity cost are not included.
MeetingWaste is a local calculator. The values you enter are processed in your browser and are not sent anywhere.
